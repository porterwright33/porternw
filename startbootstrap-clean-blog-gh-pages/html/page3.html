The Mission Experience

From July 2022 to August 2024, I served a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints across Trinidad, Tobago, and Guyana. It was the most formative experience of my life — two years of living in a different culture, learning to communicate across language and background barriers, and leading people through some of the hardest moments of their lives.

What I Learned

  1. Leadership
    • Managing logistics and personnel for an entire country
    • Coordinating healthcare and emergency response for 150+ missionaries
  2. Communication
    • Connecting with people across cultural and language barriers
    • Delivering difficult messages with compassion and clarity
  3. Resilience
    • Maintaining a 85+ hour weekly schedule for two years straight
    • Pushing forward in the face of repeated rejection and setbacks
