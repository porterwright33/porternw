About Me

Hi! I'm Porter Wright, a Business Management student at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I spent two years living in Trinidad, Tobago, and Guyana as a full-time missionary, which gave me a deep love for other cultures and a practical education in leadership and communication. Since returning, I've worked in direct sales and entrepreneurship, and I'm currently studying business with plans to go into entrepreneurship or business development.

Outside of academics and work, I love anything in the mountains, playing basketball, reading, and following how artificial intelligence is changing the world.

The mountains are my favorite place to reset and think.

My Mission in Guyana & Trinidad

From July 2022 to August 2024, I served a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints across Trinidad, Tobago, and Guyana. It was the most formative experience of my life — two years of living in a different culture, learning to communicate across language and background barriers, and leading people through some of the hardest moments of their lives.

As an assistant to the mission president, I managed logistics, healthcare coordination, and personnel oversight for an entire country — responsibilities I did not expect to carry at 20 years old.
